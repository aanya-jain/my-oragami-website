Interesting facts about a chameleon

  • Chameleons are a part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that can be wrapped around tree branches.

Interesting facts about a Pigeon

  • Pigeons are complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing power.

Interesting facts about a camel

  • Camels are the major mode of transport in a desert.
  • Camels can store water in their hump for weeks.
  • They have big eyelids to prevent sand from entering their eyes and padded feet to walk easily on sand.

Interesting facts about a teddy-bear

  • They are also stress busters.
  • They are very soft and cute and cuddley little play-things.
